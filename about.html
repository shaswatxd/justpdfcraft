About JustPDFCraft

At JustPDFCraft, we believe that powerful document tools should be accessible to everyone, without compromising privacy or security. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive, 100% free toolkit for students, professionals, and home users.

The Problem with Traditional PDF Tools

Most online PDF editors require you to upload your sensitive files to their servers. This not only takes time but also poses a massive privacy risk. You never truly know who has access to your data once it leaves your device.

Our Solution: Browser-Based Magic

We've built JustPDFCraft using cutting-edge web technologies like WebAssembly and specialized JavaScript libraries. This allows all the heavy lifting—merging, splitting, compressing, and even OCR—to happen directly in your browser. Your files never touch our servers, meaning your data remains 100% private.

Privacy First

Our Mission

We are a small team of developers passionate about open-web technologies. We want to eliminate the "gatekeeping" of basic productivity tools. Whether you're a student trying to merge assignments or a professional signing a contract, JustPDFCraft is here to help you get the job done quickly and securely.
