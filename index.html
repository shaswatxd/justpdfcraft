Why Choose JustPDFCraft?

🔒

Privacy First

Your files never leave your computer. We use browser-based processing (JavaScript/WebAssembly) to handle all operations locally. This ensures your sensitive documents remain private and secure.

⚡

Zero Wait Time

Since there's no uploading or downloading from a server, processing is near-instant. The only limit is your device's speed. Merge or compress large files in seconds.

✨

100% Free Forever

We don't believe in subscriptions or "pro" versions for basic tasks. Access every single tool, from OCR to PDF signing, without paying a single penny or creating an account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is JustPDFCraft really free?

Yes, 100%. All tools are free to use as many times as you need. We support the site through subtle, non-intrusive advertisements.

Are my files safe?

Absolutely. Unlike other sites, we don't upload your files to any server. All processing happens inside your web browser. Once you close the tab, all temporary data is gone.

Do I need to install anything?

No installation required. JustPDFCraft works directly in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) on both desktop and mobile.
