Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to JustPDFCraft. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website.

2. No File Uploads (Local Processing)

The core of JustPDFCraft is private local processing. Unlike traditional PDF tools that upload your documents to a server, our tools work entirely within your web browser using JavaScript libraries (like pdf-lib and Tesseract.js). Your documents never leave your computer, ensuring 100% data privacy for your sensitive files.

3. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information like your name, address, or document content. However, we may collect certain non-identifiable information, such as:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on the site, and which tools are used.
  • Device Info: Browser type, operating system, and screen resolution to improve the user interface.

4. Cookies and Advertising

We use cookies to improve your experience and analyze site traffic. We also use third-party services like Google AdSense to display advertisements.

  • Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

5. Data Security

Because we do not store your files on our servers, there is no risk of your document data being leaked from our infrastructure. We use standard web security practices to protect the integrity of the website itself.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR in Europe), you have rights regarding your data. Since we do not store personal data, these rights are primarily focused on your ability to control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at privacy@justpdfcraft.xyz.
